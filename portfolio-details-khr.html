My Work with Korome Hills Resort

Korome Hills Farms and Resorts, an exquisite retreat nestled in the captivating landscapes of Wayanad, Kerala, entrusted me with their digital presence. Their vision was clear: to attract a wider audience and showcase the beauty and experiences offered by their resort.As a digital marketing specialist, I was entrusted with the task of creating a visually stunning and functional website that would capture the essence of the resort. Additionally, the client sought guidance in leveraging social media platforms, specifically Facebook and Instagram, to expand their digital reach and foster brand engagement. Furthermore, enhancing the resort's online visibility through search engine optimization (SEO) and enhancing the resort's online reputation through customer reviews was a key objective.
